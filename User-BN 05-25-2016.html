Birthday

Every year I swear to disown my relatives but I never do it. My mother still calls, even after I've grown up and moved away, to talk about stupid shit (the following):


  1. My Weight
  2. Personal Trainers
  3. Trips With Her Lady Friends to Faraway Mountains

I think she's sick in the head, even more so than me, and sometimes I feel sorry for her. But mostly I ignore it until I forget to ignore it and then I hear the same shit all over again. Sometimes it's too much to bear and I get so frustrated I can't move, like my entire body is sour with anger.

"What if she's right?" This is a question that I pose to myself on my worst days. And then I remember it literally does not matter to me.


I wrote this during another heated and just overall terrible argument with my mom, but I've calmed down a bit now. Co-workers I've chatted with about it have suggested that I should maybe humor her somewhat but I think that would just end up pissing both of us off. I don't have the time for this, I have cake and booze to tear through.
