I had been there the year before too, but I had been struggling with depression back then so coming back and experiencing it in a new light was great. Like.. I went up to the top of the mountain and looking over everything and seeing the endless sky around you.. that probably added 10 years to my life. Skiing is probably one of my favorite sports now.
